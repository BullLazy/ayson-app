Code of Conduct

Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone.

By participating in this project, you agree to treat all contributors and users with respect, regardless of their experience level, background, or identity.

---

Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Provide helpful feedback.
- Keep discussions professional.
- Report bugs and security issues responsibly.
- Respect differing opinions.

---

Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or abusive language
- Personal attacks
- Trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior
- Publishing private information without permission
- Spam or malicious content

---

Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

They may remove comments, close discussions, reject contributions, or take other appropriate actions if these guidelines are violated.

---

Contact

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainers.

Thank you for helping make AYSon a welcoming project for everyone.
